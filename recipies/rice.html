Rice

Description

Rice can be eaten with almost anything or by itself. It is classic and an excellent side dish. Simple and easy, it takes only a few steps to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of uncooked rice.
  • Oil of your choice
  • Salt
  • Rice cooker

Steps

  1. Wash rice until water runs clear.
  2. Add a thin layer of oil to the bottom of the rice cooker.
  3. Place washed rice into the rice cooker.
  4. Add cold water until it is just slightly above the rice.
  5. Add some salt, close the lid, and turn on the rice cooker.
  6. Wait until cooked and serve.
