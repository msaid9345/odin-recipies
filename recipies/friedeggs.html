Fried Eggs

Description

Fried eggs are a classic meal. Usually served for breakfast with a side of bacon or sausage, this recipie will teach you the easy steps needed to make this delicous dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • Oil of your choice
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Set your stove to a low heat.
  2. Crack open the egg into the pan and wait 30 seconds to a minute until the edges of the egg become cooked.
  3. Using a spatuala or tool of your choice, flip the egg over.
  4. Based on your preference, you can leave the egg longer to get a fully cooked yoke. Once ready, move the egg onto a plate.
  5. Sprinkle on some salt and pepper. Enjoy!
